Seasonal Essentials

Holiday Gift Guide

7 December 2018, Friday

The holiday season is finally here. The lights have been strung and the cold weather has set in. I wanted to mention the products I feel deserve a place underneath your Christmas trees this year, so I've put together a well curated list. Whether it be a small gift for a friend or something a little more special for a significant other, take the time and get them something they'll absolutely love.

This list is a combination of products I enjoy myself as well as products on my own wishlist. I have divided the gifts into five different price levels so you can browse for any occassion. I hope you all can find this list helpful as you put together your gift shopping lists this season.

Gifts Under $25:

1. ASYEU Twists Socks ($14)

One of the only companies I know who focuses mainly on socks and does it well. 100% traceability and no retail markup so you know you're getting exactly what you pay for.

2. Le Labo Lip Balm ($14)

A non-sticky, non-glossy lip balm formula that is both vegan and petrolatum-free.

3. Peel Super Thin iPhone Case ($25)

Currently sporting one of these on my phone at the moment. I recommend pairing the case with their glass screen protector for optimal protection.

Gifts Under $50:

1. Aesop Resurrection Hand Balm ($27)

A blend of fragrant botanicals and skin-softening emollients that delivers rich hydration to labour-wearied hands and cuticles.

2. Legends Northern Beanie ($30)

Every piece from the brand is worth a view, but this beanie in particular is high on the list for me.

3. Universal Works Double Sided Scarf ($37)

This scarf is the perfect pop of color for any monochrome fit this winter.

4. ASKET The T-Shirt ($45)

This may be the only tee you'll ever need. Other than the standard sizing measurements, you can choose between a short, regular, and long length for a perfect fit.

Gifts Under $100:

1. Nocturna Candle ($50)

I don't usually purchase candles as they typically contain harmful toxins but these are an exception as they are vegan and ecofriendly.

2. Von Holzhausen Credit Card Holder ($55)

This card holder is entirely animal-free. It has the look and feel of real leather but is actually made of mostly recycled materials and is more durable.

3. LARQ Bottle ($95)

Probably the coolest water bottle I've ever come across. It uses UV-C LED light to purify the water and clean the bottle all on its own so you don't have to.

4. A.N Other Fragrance ($95)

I love this brand because the quality is outstanding yet the price is not inflated like most luxury parfums. All of the fragrances are also 100% vegan and cruelty-free.

Gifts Under $200:

1. Forét Spruce Sweatshirt ($107)

This is another brand on the list which deserves a full view of the collection. This is a sweatshirt than can either be dressed up or dressed down.

2. Vous Créez Houndstooth Cap ($125)

These guys make some of the best caps out there. This one in particular was just released and has to be my favorite so far.

3. L’Estrange London The 24 Trouser ($160)

I've only heard good things about these trousers. They just released the charcoal trouser but carry a black and navy pair as well.

4. Naadam The Luxe Unisex Cashmere Sweater ($195)
