Mack Weldon: The Woven Boxer

27 October 2017, Friday

I cannot stress enough how great these boxers are. This is the brand that is bringing back the traditional boxer short. I’ve never been a fan of briefs or boxer briefs; the tight fit has always felt uncomfortable to me. On the other side of that, boxer shorts are always so bulky and ugly. The Woven Boxer by Mack Weldon does away with the bad rap that the boxer short has been given for years and creates an entirely new underwear for men.

Shop Now

Its tailored fit allows for better comfort when wearing slim fit pants. Made of 98% cotton and 2% spandex, they are both breathable and bulk-free. I have searched for a solid pair of boxers for years and The Woven Boxer has outdone every boxer short out there. An added plus to purchasing from this company is that the package they send the boxers in is reusable. They offer this convenience to encourage you to send them back any or all your old undergarments including underwear, socks, and t-shirts to recycle free of charge. They may be a bit of an investment at $28 a pair, but the quality and comfort is well worth every penny.
