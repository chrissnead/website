While a lot of the world is getting back into their normal routines, I’m currently keeping quarantined as Portland is still closed for the most part. With all the time I’ve got on my hands, I’ve taken the past few months to understand my skin more to keep my face looking healthy and young with minimal breakouts. I tend to lean more towards the oily side, so my skin is much more prone to breakouts than most. Though it has lessened over time, this is something I’ve struggled with since my teen years and never really learned how to manage it correctly. If you had asked me a year ago, I’d tell you my skincare routine was a basic two-step face wash and daily moisturizer. This isn’t bad necessarily, but I’ve grown to understand the importance of products such as serums, exfoliants, and of course SUNSCREEN. So now that I am learning more about my own skin, I thought I’d share what I am currently using on a daily and why. I assume this routine will be constantly changing with some things being altered and other steps being added, but I’m pretty happy with where I’ve started and am excited to share what is working for me at the moment.
