Brand Spotlight

Farnol

7 September 2018, Friday

Farnol is a British clothing label which just launched in 2018. Founded by Mitchel Farnol, his vision has always been to create timeless pieces at an affordable price. Often times price has a reflection on quality, but not with this brand. Each product is carefully thought out to ensure the highest quality essentials.

I just recently came across Farnol a couple months ago and instantly fell in love with their collection. After browsing what they had to offer, it surprised me how affordable their prices were. I expect to hear a lot more about this company over the years considering their immediate success just within a couple months. This truly is a brand made for everyone, creating high-quality wardrobe staples at a fraction of the price. Below are a few pieces from their line which I felt stood out.
